\minisec{再来个labeling}
它能自动对齐。\\
\begin{labeling}[~~---~]{\usekomafont{descriptionlabel}myheadings}
	\item[\usekomafont{descriptionlabel}这也太长了吧]
		确实很长啊
	\item[\usekomafont{descriptionlabel}copy]
		这行是我复制上一行.
	\item[\usekomafont{descriptionlabel}vim copy很方便]
		这行同样处理了。
\end{labeling}
